User Story

User Story - Search DVDs in store

As an online shopper I am looking for a specific amount of various DVDs in DVD store.

Acceptance criteria

  • When I search for a specific DVD the amount will be shown
  • When I search for a specific DVD the title will be shown
  • When I search for a specific DVD the name of the actor will be shown
  • When I search for a specific DVD the price will be shown

End Info
