Overview

In order to order multiple items during a visit session of the shop  as shop operator we provide a shopping cart for all shop visitors.

Acceptance criteria

  • The shop visitor may only add products that are in stock
  • The shopping cart must survive the closing of the browser
  • The shopping cart is only available for registered users
  • The shopping cart is dropped after 90 min.
